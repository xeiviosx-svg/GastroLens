What is GastroLens?

GastroLens is an AI-powered app that analyzes dishes from photos.

Simply scan a dish and the app generates a structured culinary breakdown including:

  • Ingredients
  • Preparation steps
  • Cooking techniques
  • Chef tools used
  • Pairing suggestions
  • Nutrition estimates

Results are generated automatically using AI and may not always be perfectly accurate.

Getting Started

  1. Open the app
  2. Take a photo of a dish or upload an image
  3. GastroLens analyzes the dish using AI
  4. Explore the ingredients, preparation steps, and culinary insights

Accounts

To use GastroLens you need an account.

You can sign in securely using:

  • Sign in with Apple
  • Google Sign In

Your account allows you to save scans and access your scan history.

Subscription

GastroLens offers a premium subscription that unlocks additional scans and extended features.

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID account settings.

You can cancel your subscription anytime by going to:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

Free Access

New users may receive a limited free experience (such as an initial scan) before a subscription is required.

Managing Your Data

Images you scan are saved in your scan history so you can revisit previous analyses.

You can delete scans at any time within the app.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your GastroLens account and associated data directly from within the app.

Go to:

Settings → Delete Account

This action cannot be undone.
