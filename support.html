About GastroLens

GastroLens is an AI-powered culinary guidance app that helps users better understand and recreate dishes from photos.

Depending on the scan, the App may provide:

  • Dish identification
  • Ingredient and component breakdowns
  • Preparation guidance and techniques
  • Plating and pairing suggestions
  • Optional nutrition estimates

Outputs are generated automatically using AI and are provided for informational guidance only. They may not always be accurate, complete, or suitable for every situation.

Getting Started

  1. Create an account or sign in with Apple
  2. Take a photo of a dish or upload an image
  3. Review the AI-generated analysis and guidance
  4. Save, revisit, or delete scans from your account history

Accounts and Sign-In

An account is required to use most GastroLens features.

GastroLens currently supports secure authentication using Sign in with Apple only.

Sign in with Apple allows users to create an account and sign in quickly while maintaining strong privacy protections.

If you use Sign in with Apple with Apple's Hide My Email feature, Apple may provide a private relay email address.

Your account allows you to save scans, review scan history, and manage access to the App.

Free Access and Usage Limits

GastroLens may provide limited free access, such as a small number of free scans or promotional access.

Free access may be subject to daily or other usage limits. In some cases, additional access may be unlocked by optionally viewing an in-app advertisement.

Usage limits may change over time in order to maintain fair access and service stability.

Subscriptions

GastroLens offers a premium subscription that provides expanded access to the App, including higher daily scan limits and an ad-free experience.

Subscriptions are billed and managed through your Apple ID account settings. RevenueCat is used to manage subscription status and entitlements within the App.

To manage or cancel your subscription, go to:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

Advertising

GastroLens may display limited advertising within the App through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

Some advertisements may be optional and may allow users to unlock additional usage access, such as extra scans.

On iOS devices, users may be asked for permission for certain types of tracking in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

Managing Your Data

Your scans and associated AI-generated outputs are saved to your account so you can review them later.

You can delete individual scans at any time within the App.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your GastroLens account and associated data directly from within the App.

Go to:

Settings → Delete Account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
