Privacy Policy

GastroLens

Last Updated: 15-03-2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how GastroLens ("GastroLens", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the GastroLens mobile application (the "App").

GastroLens is an AI-powered culinary guidance app that allows users to upload dish photos and receive structured cooking analysis, preparation guidance, techniques, and related suggestions.

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, the data controller is HAKIM BEN YAAKOUB, Autónomo, Spain.

Contact:
contact@gastrolens.app

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to provide, secure, and improve GastroLens.

2.1 Account Information

When you create or use an account through Sign in with Apple, we may collect:

  • Authentication provider information associated with Sign in with Apple, such as the email address or unique identifier provided by Apple
  • A unique GastroLens account identifier

GastroLens currently supports Sign in with Apple for secure account authentication.

If you choose to use Sign in with Apple with Apple's Hide My Email feature, Apple may provide a private relay email address instead of your personal email address.

2.2 Uploaded Images and AI Outputs

When you use GastroLens, we process and store:

  • Images of dishes that you upload
  • The associated AI-generated analysis results
  • Related scan history linked to your account

You may delete individual scans at any time within the App.

2.3 Subscription Information

If you purchase or use a subscription, we may collect and store information such as your subscription status, entitlement information, and limited transaction metadata needed to manage access to paid features.

Payments are processed by Apple through In-App Purchases. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

2.4 Device, Usage, and Diagnostic Information

For operational, security, and performance purposes, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App usage timestamps and basic usage events
  • Crash reports and basic error logs

2.5 Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

GastroLens may display advertisements within the App using third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect limited device information, including device identifiers, IP address, and basic usage signals, in order to display advertisements and measure their performance.

On iOS devices, where required, GastroLens follows Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and asks for permission before allowing tracking that may be used for personalized advertising.

If permission is not granted, advertisements may still be displayed but may be less personalized.

For more information about how Google uses data in advertising products, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create, authenticate, and manage your account
  • Provide AI-based culinary analysis and related guidance
  • Store, display, and manage your scan history
  • Manage subscriptions, entitlements, and premium access
  • Display advertisements and measure their performance
  • Maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the App
  • Ensure security, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

Where EU data protection laws apply, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract, including providing the App and its core features
  • Legitimate interests, including security, fraud prevention, service improvement, and limited advertising operations
  • Compliance with legal obligations, including billing, tax, and regulatory requirements
  • Consent, where applicable, such as when tracking permission is required for personalized advertising on iOS

5. AI Processing and Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to operate GastroLens, including:

  • OpenAI for image analysis and AI-generated culinary guidance
  • Supabase for authentication, database services, and secure storage
  • RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Apple for Sign in with Apple and In-App Purchases
  • Advertising providers such as Google AdMob for ad delivery and measurement

When you upload an image:

  • The image is transmitted securely to our AI provider
  • The AI system analyzes the image
  • A structured response is generated and returned to the App

Processing may occur in the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

AI-generated results are automated outputs and may not always be accurate, complete, or suitable for every situation.

6. Data Storage and Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access controls, and restricted administrative access.

Uploaded images, AI-generated results, and account data are stored using our service providers' infrastructure until they are deleted by you or removed following account deletion.

No digital system can guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect the information we process.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data and stored scans while your account remains active.

You may:

  • Delete individual scans at any time
  • Delete your entire account from within the App

When you delete your account, the following are permanently deleted from our database, except where limited retention is required for legal or billing compliance:

  • All associated images
  • All AI-generated results
  • All stored preferences
  • All subscription records linked to your account

We do not automatically delete inactive accounts.

8. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the application settings.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

9. Subscription and Payments

Subscriptions are managed through Apple In-App Purchases.

  • Subscriptions may renew automatically unless canceled through Apple
  • Refunds are handled by Apple according to Apple's policies
  • GastroLens does not process or store payment card information

RevenueCat is used to manage subscription status.

10. International Data Transfers

Because we use global cloud infrastructure and AI providers, your information may be processed outside your country of residence, including in the United States.

We take reasonable measures to ensure appropriate data protection safeguards are in place.

11. Children's Privacy

GastroLens is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Your Rights Under Applicable Data Protection Law

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object (where applicable)
  • Right to withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in the EU

You can exercise your rights by contacting contact@gastrolens.app.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes are significant, we will update the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, notify users within the app.

14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact:

contact@gastrolens.app
