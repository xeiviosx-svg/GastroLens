About GastroLens

GastroLens is an AI-powered culinary guidance app that helps users understand and recreate dishes from photos.

Depending on the scan, the App may provide:

  • Dish identification
  • Ingredient and component breakdowns
  • Preparation guidance and techniques
  • Plating and pairing suggestions
  • Optional nutrition estimates

Outputs are generated automatically and provided for informational guidance only. They may not always be accurate, complete, or suitable for every situation.

Getting Started

  1. Create an account or sign in with Apple
  2. Take a photo of a dish or upload an image
  3. Review the AI-generated analysis and guidance
  4. Save, revisit, or delete recent scans from your account history

Accounts and Sign-In

An account is required for most GastroLens features.

GastroLens currently supports Sign in with Apple only.

If you use Apple's Hide My Email feature, Apple may provide a private relay email address.

Your account allows you to save scans, review recent scan history, and manage access to the App.

Free Access and Usage Limits

GastroLens currently provides up to 2 free scans.

Unlocking a free scan result may require viewing an in-app advertisement.

Usage limits may change over time in order to maintain fair access and service stability.

Subscriptions

GastroLens offers a premium subscription that currently includes up to 5 scans per day and an ad-free experience.

Subscriber features may also include saving dishes from shared links or importing shared dishes, subject to limits shown in the App.

Subscription features and limits may change over time and are shown in the App.

Subscriptions are billed and managed through your Apple ID account settings. RevenueCat is used to manage subscription status and entitlements.

To manage or cancel your subscription, go to:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

Advertising

GastroLens may display limited advertising in the App through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

Certain free features may require viewing an in-app advertisement, including unlocking scan results.

On iOS devices, users may be asked for permission for certain types of tracking in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

Managing Your Data

Your scans and associated AI-generated outputs are saved to your account so you can review them later.

GastroLens stores up to 50 of the most recent scans per account.

When you create a new scan after that limit is reached, the oldest stored scan is automatically removed from your account. The related image and AI-generated output are permanently deleted from our systems and cannot be recovered through the App.

You can delete individual scans at any time in the App.

Sharing and Community

Some features may allow you to share dishes and AI-generated outputs through shared links or community features such as trending displays.

Subscribers may save or import shared dishes, subject to a combined limit of up to 7 saved or imported dishes per day.

This limit helps protect the service and prevent abuse. Current limits may change over time and are shown in the App.

If you use these features, the related content may be visible to others and may be accessed or imported through the App.

If you need help with shared content or would like to report content for review, contact contact@gastrolens.app.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your GastroLens account and associated data from within the App.

Go to:

Settings → Delete Account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
